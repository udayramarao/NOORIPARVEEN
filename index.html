OUR IMPACT & WORK

Discover the real difference we make through our focused initiatives. Each area reflects our commitment to compassion, empowerment, and sustainable change.

HEALTH CAMPS

We have helped many poor people by conducting various health camps, providing free medical check-ups and distributing essential medicines. Our dedicated medical teams reach remote areas to ensure no one is left behind in receiving healthcare.

WOMEN EMPOWERMENT

We empower women by providing books, clothes, and motivational talks. Our programs aim to boost confidence, encourage education, and create new opportunities for women to thrive in society.

ENVIRONMENT

Our team actively plants trees and organizes clean-up drives to protect and restore the environment. We believe in sustainable living and work with communities to reduce waste and promote green practices.

FEEDING ANIMALS

We care for stray and abandoned animals by providing regular feeding programs and medical care. Our volunteers ensure that animals in need receive compassion and support.

COVID-19 PANDEMIC

During the COVID-19 crisis, we helped many people by providing free medications, sanitizers, and masks. Our rapid response teams worked tirelessly to support vulnerable communities and raise awareness about safety measures.

ORPHANS

We are committed to taking care of many orphans by providing shelter, education, and emotional support. Our mission is to give every child a loving environment and the tools they need to succeed in life.

Dr. Noori Parveen: The ₹10 Doctor with a Heart of Gold

In the heart of India, where hope often battles hardship, shines the inspiring story of Dr. Noori Parveen, fondly known as the “₹10 Doctor.” A General Physician by profession and a humanitarian by heart, Dr. Noori has been a beacon of light for the underprivileged since 2016.

Raised with values rooted in compassion and service, Noori always dreamed of healing more than just bodies—she wanted to heal communities. In 2020, after years of dedicated work, she officially registered her dream as Noori’s Hospitals, a charitable trust aimed at providing affordable healthcare to all. Her unique initiative of charging just ₹10 for consultations earned her the title “₹10 Doctor,” a symbol of accessible healthcare for the poor.

But Dr. Noori’s impact reaches far beyond hospital walls.

She runs an orphanage home, offering love, education, and shelter to children who have no one else. Her hands, though constantly busy healing patients, also find time to plant trees, clean streets, and care for animals. Through environmental campaigns and awareness drives, she has nurtured not only lives but also the earth.

From organizing free health camps in remote villages to distributing books and clothes to the needy, Noori’s life is a living example of selflessness. During the harrowing days of the COVID-19 pandemic, while many stayed behind closed doors, she stepped out—fearless and determined. She treated thousands, offered food and essentials to struggling families, and gave a voice to the voiceless.

Her story doesn’t end there. Dr. Noori regularly holds motivational talks for poor children and struggling communities, encouraging them to believe in education, hope, and hard work. To them, she’s not just a doctor—she’s a guide, a sister, a savior.

In a world often driven by profit, Dr. Noori Parveen stands as a powerful reminder that kindness still exists. Her work is a living testimony that one woman with a strong will and a compassionate heart can change countless lives.

She is not just a doctor.
She is a movement.
She is Dr. Noori Parveen—the doctor who treats with medicine and heals with love.
